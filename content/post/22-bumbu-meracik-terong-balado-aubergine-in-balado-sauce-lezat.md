##### Tata cara mengolah Terong balado (aubergine in balado sauce):

1. Chop the aubergines, i prefer to have smallish chunks as they fry and cook faster.
1. Fry the aubergines.
1. Grind everything except for the aubergines into a paste. I’d recommend doing the garlic, shallots and terasi first and ensure it is smoothish. Then do the tomatoes (prediced), with the chilles.
1. Get your wok/frying pan add oil, put the lemon grass and lime leaves along with the chilli paste into the wok and fry until the paste is cooked and very fragrant, add salt, stock granules, and sugar until flavour is correct. Put the fried aubergines in the sauce and combine, be gentle as aubergines are very soft.
